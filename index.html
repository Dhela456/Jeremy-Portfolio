About

Building Intelligence from Data

I'm a data scientist passionate about uncovering the stories hidden inside complex datasets. My work covers the full ML pipeline — from exploratory analysis and feature engineering, through model development and rigorous evaluation, to visualization and stakeholder-ready output.

I've applied these skills across pharmaceutical analytics, retail demand forecasting, customer segmentation, quality prediction, and real estate market analysis.

  • Predictive modeling — regression, classification, ensemble methods
  • Time series forecasting — ARIMA, Prophet, feature engineering
  • Unsupervised learning — K-Means clustering, PCA, dimensionality reduction
  • Exploratory data analysis and statistical modeling
  • Interactive dashboards and data storytelling
